Prasad V Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology (PVPSIT)

Overview

Prasad V Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology (PVPSIT) is a
private engineering college located in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh,
India. The institution was established in 1998 and is managed by the
Siddhartha Academy of General and Technical Education.

Location

The campus is situated in Kanuru, Vijayawada, which is one of the major
educational hubs in Andhra Pradesh.

Affiliation and Accreditation

PVPSIT is affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University,
Kakinada (JNTUK).
The college is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education
(AICTE).
Several programs are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation
(NBA).

Courses Offered

The institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in
engineering and technology. Some of the major branches include: -
Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) - Information Technology (IT) -
Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) - Electrical and
Electronics Engineering (EEE) - Mechanical Engineering - Civil
Engineering - Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

Postgraduate programs such as M.Tech and MBA are also offered.

Facilities

The college provides several facilities to support student learning and
development: - Modern laboratories - Central library with digital
resources - Computer centers - Sports facilities - Hostel
accommodation - Seminar halls and auditoriums

Placements

PVPSIT has an active placement cell that helps students secure jobs in
various companies. The placement cell conducts training programs,
workshops, and campus recruitment drives with leading IT and core
companies.

Campus Life

Students at PVPSIT participate in various technical, cultural, and
sports activities. The college organizes fests, hackathons, seminars,
and workshops throughout the academic year to encourage student
innovation and skill development.

Conclusion

PVPSIT is considered one of the well-known engineering colleges in
Vijayawada, providing quality technical education and opportunities for
students to develop both academically and professionally.
